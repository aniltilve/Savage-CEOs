Young Chuck

Follow Me

Bio

When Young Chuck was 15 he started breakdancing, learning different power moves. in school he met with others with the same passion for dancing that he had. Forming a group in high school, Chucky competed against other dance groups in his town and by going to Bboy cyphers and battles across the Tri-state area. Eventually he met with a high profile dancer at a social that saw potential in him. Thus he was initiated into Souljerz dance crew which travels nationwide performing at various locations for large crowds such as the Super Bowl, the US Open, and the MLB All Star Pre Game Show.
