Bio

NicKey Stringz, producer and artist from Brooklyn, NY. His is half Jamaican and half ChineseSelf taught musician that started with a casio keyboard. He eventually taught himself how to play the keys on a grand piano. He then went on to learn guitar and drums as well. NicKey is a renaissance man when it comes to music. His ability to amplify emotion and rhythm is moving.

About NicKey Stringz

"The music gene was planted in me from my pops, who used to DJ. He was spinning records, I was born in that, raised in that. It was soulful, deep, and profound, so profound, that I tried to get away from it. After a few years had passed, life brought me full circle and I came back to music. I make music where you feel, if you can't feel that it's not music."
