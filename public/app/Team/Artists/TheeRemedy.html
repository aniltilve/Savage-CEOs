Bio

Artist from Fort Wayne, IN. I do music because I know how it's helped me, so I want to be that artist that understands what people are going through. I want my music to heal people.

About Thee Remedy

I grew up with a very broad spectrum of music in my life and from a young age it's always been able to help me express myself. I write poetry and of course my music. I have 3 children, 2 girls and a boy, and they're my world.
