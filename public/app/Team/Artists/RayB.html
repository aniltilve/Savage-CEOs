Bio

In the beginning Ray B used to freestyle battle with his classmates in middle school. He later released his first two mixtapes in 2011 with Gotta Grind Entertainment music group in Indianapolis. Ray B currently resides in Indiana, he has performed live on-stage in Peru, Indiana, Indianapolis, Indiana, and his hometown of Fort Wayne, Indiana.

Upcoming Music

His solo debut was in 2015 when he released his first mixtape called Euphoria Music. He plans to work very hard on recording new tracks for his future projects..
